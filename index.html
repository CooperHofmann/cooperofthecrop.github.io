About COOPEROFTHECROP

Founded in 2018, COOPEROFTHECROP is a photography studio specializing in brutalist and minimalist compositions that highlight the raw essence of subjects.

Our approach strips away the unnecessary, focusing on form, texture, and the interplay of light and shadow.

Based in New York, working worldwide.
